The Best Games According to Bharat

My top 5 games of all time

Red Dead Redemption 2

This is one of the most detailed and a story rich open world game.

Gta V

One of the most famous game of gta series. Unlike other gta games this has 3 protagonists having their own back-story.

Batman Arkham Knight

This is one of the most action packed open world batman game, where the batman(Dark knight) is on the verge of saving the city from the chaotic criminals.

Assassin's creed Odyssey

Assassin's creed odyssey is the first game among the assassin's creed series which takes place in Egypt. The developers build the city of pyramids in a so convincing way so that this game is captivating the gamers, forcing them to play for a longer duration of time.

Outlast

Outlast is a psychological horror genre game in which the protagonist who is on the mission to disclose the secrets of Mount Massive Asylum where the dangerous and illegal experiments being conducted on mental patients has to escpae through the hands of pyschotic patients and doctors and run his way through all the diificulties and massive horror to save his own life.
